Project Description

To create an illustrative AR/VR website that specializing in augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR) technology for interactive experiences and immersive storytelling which showcases capabilities, engages visitors with interactive elements, and drives interest from potential clients in various industries.

Challenges:
  • Conveying Immersive Experience:
    The website needed to give visitors a taste of the immersive experience that AR and VR technologies can provide, despite being viewed on standard devices.
  • Creating Engaging Visuals:
    The challenge was to design captivating and interactive illustrations that would align with the company's brand identity and effectively showcase their AR/VR capabilities.
  • Intuitive Navigation:
    With interactive elements, it was essential to strike a balance between creativity and ensuring intuitive navigation for all users.
Solution
  • Illustrative Design Language:
    Created a unique illustrative design language that embodied the brand identity. The illustrations combined 2D elements, giving visitors a glimpse of the immersive experiences they could expect.
  • Engaging Storytelling:
    To captivate visitors, we incorporated storytelling elements throughout the website. The use of AR/VR in various settings, such as gaming, training, and marketing campaigns, was presented as engaging case studies.
  • Responsive Design:
    As AR/VR technologies are not limited to specific devices, the website was developed with responsive design principles to ensure accessibility across various platforms, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
